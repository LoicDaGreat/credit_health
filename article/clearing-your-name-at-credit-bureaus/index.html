How To Clear Your Name At Credit Bureaus.

Check your credit report and clear your name.

These days it is easy to get your credit report online. Even if you don't have bad credit, you might want to check your report because it's important to know what's on there. It's also important to know what items you don't want to see on your credit report. If you have bad credit, there are steps you can take to improve your credit score.

Your credit report

Even if you have good credit, you should stay on top of your credit report because you never know what could happen. Failing to pay off a loan could mean that, when you're ready to settle down, you won't qualify for a bond. Always check your credit report for any inaccuracies, and to ensure you have a good credit score.

Improve your credit score

Here are three ways you can improve your credit score:

  • Pay off all your debts: The easiest way to remove a financial black mark on your credit report is to pay your debt in full. A creditor is legally obligated to report when you settle an account in full and will likely do so before accepting installments.
  • Go into debt counseling: Debt consolidation is best done by working with an established financial professional to organise all of your various debts into one manageable monthly payment. It’s important to ensure that you’re contracting with a trustworthy service provider for this kind of help. There are consumer protections in place meant to safeguard against unscrupulous agents who might take your money and run without completing the agreed upon work.
  • Always check your credit score: Knowing your credit score and the details contained in your credit report, will help you start making smart choices about your finances.

You can clear your name at credit bureaus. Contact Credit Health for more advice on how to get out of debt, and improve your credit score.
